Things to do

Now is the perfect time to see the Middle East differently. Savor the rich flavors of our diverse cuisine, or explore our ancient cities through guided tours or vibrant street art walks. Dance under the stars at outdoor concerts and traditional festivals, or immerse yourself in history at ancient forts and archaeological sites. Whether you want to laugh along to comedy in Dubai’s entertainment scene, explore unique museums and cultural hubs, or enjoy a relaxing evening with a traditional Bedouin feast, let your journey of discovering the Middle East start right here.

Where?

Dubai

A modern metropolis with stunning skyscrapers, world-class shopping, and thrilling desert adventures.

Muscat

With its rich history, serene beaches, and majestic mountains, Muscat is a perfect blend of culture and nature.

Doha

A vibrant city where traditional culture meets modern innovation, offering a unique blend of art, shopping, and cuisine.
